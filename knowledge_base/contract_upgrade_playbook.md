# Contract Upgrade Playbook

Contract structure is a strong retention lever when churn risk is driven by low commitment and short tenure.

Best-fit signals:
- Month-to-month plan
- Tenure below 12 months
- Moderate to high churn score without severe support issues

Recommended actions:
- Present a one-year plan with a modest loyalty incentive
- Explain the stability benefit in simple business language
- Bundle the contract upgrade with one additional value feature

Avoid:
- Pushing a long contract when the customer is already frustrated with service quality
- Forcing a contract change without a meaningful value exchange
