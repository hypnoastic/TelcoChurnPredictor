# Payment Friction Playbook

Payment method and billing experience can be a hidden churn driver, especially for customers using manual or failure-prone workflows.

Best-fit signals:
- Electronic check payment method
- Paperless billing confusion
- Irregular payment history or billing complaints

Recommended actions:
- Offer autopay migration with clear billing transparency
- Reconfirm billing cycle, due dates, and expected charges
- Pair billing clarification with a small loyalty incentive only if cost sensitivity is clear

Avoid:
- Treating billing complaints as pure pricing issues
- Adding promotional complexity that makes the bill harder to understand
