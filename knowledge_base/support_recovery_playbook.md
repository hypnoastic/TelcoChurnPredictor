# Support Recovery Playbook

Customers with repeated friction usually churn because trust drops before price becomes the main issue.

Best-fit signals:
- No tech support subscription
- Service protection features disabled
- Complaints about reliability or unresolved issues

Recommended actions:
- Prioritize a proactive support callback or troubleshooting session
- Add temporary premium support or device protection trial access
- Confirm resolution and schedule a follow-up within a few days

Avoid:
- Sending only marketing offers without resolving the underlying issue
- Waiting for the customer to open the next ticket before intervening
