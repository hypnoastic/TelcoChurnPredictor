# Digital Adoption Playbook

Customers with limited product adoption often churn because they never reach sticky usage habits.

Best-fit signals:
- Low adoption of security, backup, or protection add-ons
- Newer customers still in early onboarding
- Usage breadth lower than peers on similar plans

Recommended actions:
- Recommend one or two relevant add-ons instead of the full catalog
- Use onboarding-style education and guided activation steps
- Emphasize outcomes such as security, convenience, or fewer service interruptions

Avoid:
- Cross-selling too many features in one intervention
- Using generic messaging that does not connect to the customer profile
