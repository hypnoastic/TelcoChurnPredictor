# Pricing Retention Playbook

Use pricing-led retention only when billing pressure is visible in the churn signal.

Best-fit signals:
- Higher monthly charges with low perceived bundle value
- Month-to-month contract with short tenure
- Payment complaints or cost-sensitivity in customer notes

Recommended actions:
- Offer a time-boxed discount instead of a permanent price cut
- Re-bundle services to increase value perception before lowering price
- Tie the offer to a contract upgrade or loyalty period

Avoid:
- Giving the deepest discount as a first action
- Discounting customers whose main issue is service quality or support frustration
