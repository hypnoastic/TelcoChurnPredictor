# High-Value Customer Playbook

High-value customers should receive retention plans that protect revenue and preserve trust.

Best-fit signals:
- Higher monthly charges and multiple active services
- Medium or high churn risk
- Long enough tenure to justify white-glove treatment

Recommended actions:
- Use a personalized outreach plan instead of a generic campaign
- Sequence actions: diagnosis first, then targeted offer, then follow-up check
- Assign a clear owner from retention or customer success

Avoid:
- Sending the same intervention used for low-value accounts
- Offering broad discounts without understanding the main risk driver
